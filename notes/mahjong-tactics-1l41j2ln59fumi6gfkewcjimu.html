Safe Discards

A good way to see what is safe to discard, is by seeing what your opponents discards.

If they discard a tile like 4. then it's less likely they have a 56 run and a 23 run, thus 7 is safer.

To illustrate this point better let's look at the concrete example of a 4 discard

As we can see here, the 4 discard, means that a 23 run and a 56 run are less likely, thus discarding a 1 or 7 is much safer
